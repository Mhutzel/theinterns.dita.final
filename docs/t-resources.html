Internship Resources

Want to discover different internship resources?

This page provides some of the best outlets to find Internships, whether it be through Virginia Tech, or an Outside source

Through Virginia Tech

  • Hokies4Hire: This link takes you to the log in page for Hokies4Hire, once logged in you can navigate job opportunities, career events, modify your profile and much more.

  • Career Fairs: Here is a link to the Virginia Tech career fair/job fair page, here you can find information on department specific career fairs, including dates, locations and more.

  • Career Shift: Career shift is a free outlet for Virginia Tech students to market themselves to millions of jobs. This site provides in-depth access to company information as well as millions of resume and cover letter templates to work with.

  • GoinGlobal: This Link will take you to the Virginia Tech login page for GoinGlobal, input your VT PID and password to gain access to career guides, jobs, internships and more.

  • Career Spots Video: Scroll through multiple help videos that cover topics from building a resume to proper interview dress attire.

  • Candid Career: Candid Career offers hundreds of interviews from professionals in their respective fields.

Internship opportunities can be found in many different locations, not just through Virginia Tech. Here is a list of some of the most popular internship outlets.

Outside Sources

  • Indeed.com: Indeed is one of the most popular online job listing sites. It is home to millions of Internships, full-time, and part-times jobs as well as much more. Through this link, put in the position you are looking for and the area in which you want to work, and begin searching.

  • Internships.com: Another popular site for students to find internships is no other place than Internships.com, Make sure to create a student account to get the most out of this site.

  • Simplyhired.com: Everything you need to know about simplyhired.com is in the name, This site offers millions of job listings for a variety of different fields.
